<h1 class="code-line" data-line-start="0" data-line-end="1">Voice Star</h1>
<h2 class="code-line" data-line-start="1" data-line-end="2"> 
The voice star tool is all about identifying the strenghs of each team member and understanding the best way to work together. The goal being to define roles for indivdual members.
<p class="has-line-data" data-line-start="2" data-line-end="3">The voice star identifies five core skills for radical innovation. Each member should qualitatively rank themselves on each trait from the least (at the center) to the greatest (at the tip). This tool guides teams on how to assign tasks and understand expectations moving forward.</p>
<h4 class="code-line" data-line-start="4" data-line-end="5">Voracious</h4>
<ul>
<li class="has-line-data" data-line-start="5" data-line-end="7">
<p>Voraciousness is defined by internal drive and eagerness. Voracious people are highly motivated and desire to make a large impact in their ideas and their implementation. This drive can be seen both in the quantity and quality of outcomes.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="code-line" data-line-start="7" data-line-end="8">Open</h4>
<ul>
<li class="has-line-data" data-line-start="8" data-line-end="10">
<p>Having an open mindset ensures that this individual will recognize big ideas and the steps necessary to realize those ambitions. A good thing to ask is "why not?" It can be the most impactful ideas that seem outlandish which can make the greatest impact. Tolerance towards new ideas means that this indivdual can handle big picture tasks and concepts.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="code-line" data-line-start="10" data-line-end="11">Instigates</h4>
<ul>
<li class="has-line-data" data-line-start="11" data-line-end="13">
<p>The instigator is the catalyst for action to happen! This indivdual asks the big questions and motivate their peers to think and act upon them. Effective ideas require radical thinking to spur innovative practices. The instigator is a motivated individual who is driven to make big initial impacts.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="code-line" data-line-start="13" data-line-end="14">Curious</h4>
<ul>
<li class="has-line-data" data-line-start="11" data-line-end="13">
<p>Curiousness is all about reaching out for inquisitive ideas. The curious person is not afraid to ask questions that noone else will! There are never enough questions to be answered to curious people, one answer always leads to more questions.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4 class="code-line" data-line-start="13" data-line-end="14">Earnest</h4>
<ul>
<li class="has-line-data" data-line-start="11" data-line-end="13">
<p class="has-line-data" data-line-start="20" data-line-end="21">Being earnest is all about having the strengh to be honest. Clear evaluation of ideas and prospectives is an important step to understanding why things are the way they are. The earnest person is serious and intense in their applications of judgement about what is and is not the right way to do things.</p>
</li>
</ul>
